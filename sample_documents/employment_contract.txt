EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on January 15, 2024, between TechCorp Industries, a Delaware corporation ("Company"), and Sarah Johnson ("Employee").

POSITION AND DUTIES

Employee is hired for the position of Senior Software Engineer in the Engineering Department. Employee will report directly to the Vice President of Engineering and will be responsible for:

- Developing and maintaining software applications
- Participating in code reviews and technical discussions
- Mentoring junior developers
- Contributing to architectural decisions
- Following company policies and procedures

COMPENSATION

Base Salary: Employee will receive an annual base salary of $120,000, payable in bi-weekly installments of $4,615.38, subject to applicable withholdings.

Performance Bonus: Employee is eligible for an annual performance bonus of up to 20% of base salary, based on individual and company performance metrics.

Stock Options: Employee will receive stock options to purchase 5,000 shares of Company common stock at the current fair market value, vesting over four years with a one-year cliff.

BENEFITS

Health Insurance: Company provides comprehensive health insurance coverage including medical, dental, and vision. Employee contribution is $150 per month for individual coverage.

Retirement Plan: Company offers a 401(k) retirement plan with company matching up to 6% of salary.

Life Insurance: Company provides life insurance coverage equal to 2x annual salary at no cost to Employee.

Disability Insurance: Short-term and long-term disability insurance provided by Company.

LEAVE POLICIES

Vacation Time: Employee is entitled to 20 days of paid vacation per year, accruing at 1.67 days per month. Unused vacation may be carried over up to 5 days annually.

Sick Leave: Employee receives 10 days of paid sick leave per year. Sick leave does not carry over to the following year.

Personal Days: Employee is entitled to 3 personal days per year for personal matters.

Parental Leave: Employee is eligible for 12 weeks of paid parental leave following the birth or adoption of a child.

Bereavement Leave: Employee is entitled to 3 days of paid bereavement leave for immediate family members.

WORKING CONDITIONS

Work Schedule: Full-time position requiring 40 hours per week, generally Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

Remote Work: Employee may work remotely up to 2 days per week with manager approval.

Overtime: Non-exempt employees receive overtime pay at 1.5x regular rate for hours worked over 40 per week.

TERMINATION

At-Will Employment: This is an at-will employment relationship. Either party may terminate this agreement at any time with or without cause.

Notice Period: Employee agrees to provide 2 weeks written notice before voluntary termination.

Severance: If terminated by Company without cause, Employee will receive severance pay equal to 4 weeks of base salary.

CONFIDENTIALITY AND NON-DISCLOSURE

Employee acknowledges access to confidential and proprietary information and agrees to:
- Maintain strict confidentiality of all proprietary information
- Not disclose confidential information to third parties
- Return all company property upon termination
- Not compete with Company for 6 months after termination within 50-mile radius

INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

All work product, inventions, and intellectual property created during employment belongs to Company. Employee assigns all rights to Company for work-related creations.

DISPUTE RESOLUTION

Any disputes arising from this Agreement will be resolved through binding arbitration in accordance with Delaware state law.

ENTIRE AGREEMENT

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior negotiations, representations, or agreements.

GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement is governed by Delaware state law.
